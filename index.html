Research topics

Variability in the Be Stars

I've been working on the variability of the disk in Be Stars with Dr. Catalina Arcos in the Massive Stars Group for a year and a half, where I developed codes that read the FITS and TXT files to prepare the data in order to obtain the correct measurements of the stellar parameters of the lines.

  • Massive Stars Group

Quiescence State of Dwarf Novae

At the start of the second half of 2020, I joined the Dwarf Novae Team, led by Dr. Nikolaus Vogt. Our goal is to study the quiescence state of dwarf novae taking the data of GAIA alerts in the VSX database. My contribution was to make a code to performance the period variation in phase curves from light curves from the DAT files.
